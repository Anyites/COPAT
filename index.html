COPAT by LADA-Bytes

CRUDE OIL PRODUCTION ANALYSIS TOOL

FULLY RESPONSIVE INTERACTIVE DATA MAP

COPAT focuses on providing easy to understand visualizations of geographical/historical data on the production of crude oil in the United States. The interactive map can be used to compare independent/dependent sets of data to speed up the process of larger analytical projects.
